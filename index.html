About

Animefest 2022, a time and place where animanga lovers, artists, and entertainers celebrate what we love! On February 12, 2022, participants go to the Price Center at UCSD to attend discussion and workshop panels, buy the best artwork, and check out your favorite manga in our manga library! And of course, there will be cosplays, festival games, and video games—all for some otaku fun.

Main Stage

Our annual Cosplay Masquerade and raffles are held on the Main Stage in West Ballroom.

Exhibitor Hall

Browse fanmade merchandise drawn or designed by local artists in the Artist Alley, or check out items sold by industry vendors.

Panels

We have informational and interactive panels for your enjoyment run by students, alumni, and professionals. Check them out!

Performances and Showings

Performances and movie showings will be held in the Price Center Theater.

Gaming

We have a Super Smash Bros Ultimate tournament with prizes, brought to you by Triton Smash!

Tabletop Games and Origami

Have fun with activities hosted by the Board Game and Origami Folders clubs.

Manga Library

Relax and check out a book from Cal Animage Beta's library of donated manga.

Map and Schedule

Animefest is held at the Price Center at UC San Diego. Specifically, it will be at the West Ballroom, Theater, Thurgood Marshall, Eleanor Roosevelt, Roger Revelle, Bear, Red Shoe, and Green Table rooms.

All rooms are located on the second floor with the exception of the Theater, which is on the first floor and below the Bear Room.
