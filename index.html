REGISTRATION

Admission to Animefest is free for all UCSD affiliates (those with an @ucsd.edu email)! UCSD affiliates includes undergrads, grad students, employees, and alumni. All attendees must register at one of our check-in tables designated on the map in the section below.

All attendees will also have to present a completed Event Participant Waiver for UC San Diego events. This waiver can be electronically signed prior to the convention using this link. We strongly recommend all attendees complete the waiver ahead of time. There will be another form to fill out at check-in on the day of Animefest.

PARKING

Unfortunately, parking is not free on UCSD's campus. We recommend you find free parking off campus and walking to campus or taking the bus to campus. We apologize for the inconvenience.

For parking on campus, please take a look at the official campus map, expand Parking by Permit Type, and select Visitor in the menu to see areas where you can park. Please ensure that you park in designated Visitor spots which will be marked on the floor in these areas.

Please consult this document for guides on where to park and how to acquire a permit.
