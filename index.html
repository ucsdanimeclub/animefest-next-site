ABOUT

Animefest, a time and place where animanga lovers, artists, and entertainers celebrate what we love! On February 14, 2026, participants go to the Price Center at UCSD to attend discussion and workshop panels, buy the best artwork, and check out your favorite manga in our manga library! And of course, there will be cosplays, festival games, and video games—all for some otaku fun.

REGISTRATION

Admission to Animefest is free for all UCSD affiliates (those with an @ucsd.edu email)! UCSD affiliates includes undergrads, grad students, employees, and alumni. For attendees who are not UCSD affiliates, there will be a $5 general entry fee. All attendees must register at one of our check-in tables on the day of the event.

PARKING

Unfortunately, parking is not free on UCSD's campus. We recommend you find free parking off campus and walking to campus or taking the bus to campus. We apologize for the inconvenience.

For parking on campus, please take a look at the official campus map, expand Parking by Permit Type, and select Visitor in the menu to see areas where you can park. Please ensure that you park in designated Visitor spots which will be marked on the floor in these areas.

Please consult this document for guides on where to park and how to acquire a permit.
