PERFORMANCES

ABOUT

Performances will be held in the East Ballroom. Check out our featured performers below!

UCSD WUSHU CLUB

UCSD Wushu Club's Website

12:00 PM - 12:15 PM: Performance

We are a club dedicated to passing on and promoting the culture of Chinese Wushu, a performance-based contemporary martial art. We're thrilled to have the opportunity to perform martial arts featuring characters and music from anime, games, and comics at Animefest 2024. Check out our Instagram @UCSDWushu or join our Discord!

MOCHI CAFE

Mochi Cafe's Website

12:30 PM - 1:00 PM: Performance

Mochi Cafe is a San Diego-based maid cafe seeking to bring a taste of Akihabara to you!~✿ Our team of maids and butlers take pride in putting smiles on our guests' faces with high-energy dance performances, colorful uniforms, and lots of games to play right at your table!

HOPKINS SAX JAM

Hopkins Sax Jam's Website

1:15 PM - 1:35 PM: Performance

Hopkins Sax Jam, or 'HopSax' for short, is a group of student musicians brought together by a passion for the saxophone. We play music from a variety of genres and pop culture: jazz, anime, video games, you name it!

INTERMISSION ORCHESTRA

Intermission Orchestra's Website

3:00 PM - 3:30 PM: Performance

The Intermission Orchestra at UC San Diego is a student-run orchestra dedicated to bringing the music from video games, anime, and films to life! In preparation to the much-anticipated AnimeFest 2024, we've prepared an array of selections for your favorite animes, and we're beyond excited to perform for everyone!

SUPER TONIC

Super Tonic's Website

4:30 PM - 5:30 PM: Performance

Super Tonic is a San Diego based anime and video game cover band formed in 2019 from UCSD Alumni. They love anime, video games, and music so they joined together to creatively combine their passions and share it with the world.
